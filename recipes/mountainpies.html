Mountain Pies

I'll bet you can't beat these Mountain Pies

Ingredients

  • sandwich bread
  • butter
  • pizza sauce
  • mozzarella
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. Have a cleaned pie iron open and ready.
  2. Spread an even layer of butter or margarine on one side of two pieces of bread.
  3. Place the bread butter side down into the mountain pie maker.
  4. Layer the fillings on top of the bread being careful to not get too close to the edges of the bread.
  5. Add the second piece of bread butter side up.
  6. Add the top of the pie iron and clip it shut.
  7. Cook over coals and not the flames.
